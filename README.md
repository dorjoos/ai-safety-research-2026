# Latest Advances in AI Safety Research 2026

A comprehensive survey of AI safety techniques, alignment research, and prompt injection defenses published in 2026.

## Authors
- Dr. Sarah Chen, Stanford AI Lab
- Dr. Marcus Williams, Stanford AI Lab

## Abstract
This paper surveys recent developments in AI safety, with focus on prompt injection defenses, alignment techniques, and the emerging challenges posed by agentic AI systems. We review 47 papers published in 2025-2026 and propose a taxonomy of defense mechanisms.

## Key Topics
- Indirect prompt injection defense mechanisms
- AI alignment strategies
- Agentic AI system security
- LLM safety benchmarks (BIPIA, AIR-Bench)
- Defense-in-depth approaches
